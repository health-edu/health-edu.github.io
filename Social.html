Types of Health Education

Social health

Social Health is a term that refers to the ways in which people create healthy and positive interpersonal
relationships with one another. Having good social health helps people improve their emotional wellbeing and
feel supported in their daily lives.

An example of social health is the amount of interaction a person has with their community. An example of
social health for a society is laws and regulations being applied to all citizens equally. An example
of social health is public access to the decision-making processes.

Treating others with respect. Maintaining and building strong relationships with friends. Creating healthy
boundaries that help with communication, trust and conflict management. Turning to friends and family for support.
